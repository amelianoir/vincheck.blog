# Top 10 Things to Check Before Buying a Used Car

Don't get scammed! Follow this checklist to ensure you're getting a good deal.

## 1. ✅ VIN Check (Most Important!)

Run a full VIN history report to uncover:
- Accidents
- Theft records
- Mileage rollbacks
- Title brands

[Get your VIN report here](https://epicvin.com?a_aid=0xhataau2iwvr)

## 2. 🔍 Exterior Inspection

Look for:
- **Rust** (especially wheel wells and undercarriage)
- **Paint mismatches** (sign of bodywork)
- **Panel gaps** (indicates previous collision)

## 3. 🚗 Test Drive

Pay attention to:
- Strange noises (grinding, squealing)
- Vibrations in steering wheel
- Brake responsiveness
- Transmission shifts smoothly

## 4. 🛠️ Mechanical Inspection

Hire a mechanic to check:
- Engine compression
- Transmission fluid
- Suspension components
- Exhaust system

## 5. 📄 Service Records

Request:
- Oil change history
- Major repairs
- Recall work completed

## 6. 💰 Fair Market Value

Use tools like:
- Kelley Blue Book
- Edmunds
- NADA Guides

## 7. 🔐 Title Verification

Ensure the title is:
- **Clean** (not salvage or rebuilt)
- In the seller's name
- Free of liens

## 8. 🧪 Emissions Test

Check if the car passes:
- State emissions standards
- OBD-II diagnostics

## 9. 📸 Documentation

Take photos of:
- VIN plate
- Odometer reading
- Any damage
- Seller's ID

## 10. 🤝 Negotiation

Use findings to:
- Lower the price
- Request repairs
- Walk away if needed

## Final Thoughts

A VIN check is your **#1 defense** against fraud. Don't skip it!
